Tripinium provides useful tips for your next trip. These would be tips around what to pack for your trip, peak season months, options for local travel, monuments to visit, things to do, currency conversion outlets, links to particularly insightful reviews on other websites, etc. Our intention is to provide tips which a local should be able to provide. We bring a local perspective to global travel planning. We analyse the content available on the internet and create a list of useful tips while also providing relevant links to detailed information present elsewhere on the internet.

How is it different from Tripadvisor, Lonely Planet, or even Foursquare ?
While most of these websites are destinations for travel planning, we are more like gateways for travel planning - think of a specialised search engine for travel planning - although that goal is very ambitious at present. We provide actionable, practical tips for travel planners, and point them to other detailed resources relevant to their task rather than provide encyclopaedic information ourselves. We are infact trying to complement these great services by being an effective gateway.
