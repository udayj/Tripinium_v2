How does Tripinium work ?

Tripinium offers advice to travel planners on the last minute details for their next trip. To this end, it utilises deep local knowledge to prepare a carefully curated list of things that can help you enjoy your trip to the fullest. We provide you with useful tips that can help you organize the little details of your trip like where to eat, time to avoid the peak hour rush to your favorite tourist destination, how to commence a conversation with a foreigner, etc. We work hard so that you can travel smart .

What is your team ?

We are a bunch of guys trying to make travel planning simple. We have expertise to draw from graduate work at IIM-K, and work experience at companies like Microsoft, Amazon, etc.
